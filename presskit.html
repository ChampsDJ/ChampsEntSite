CHAMPS

DJ | Producer | Entertainer

Bio

Champs is a Pittsburgh-based DJ and producer known for high-energy sets blending house, techno, and trance. He has rocked stages at venues like Bottlerocket Social Hall, Spirit Lodge, and Hellbender Vinyl, and delivered guest mixes on Ibiza Stardust Radio, MSYH.FM, and Elevated Eclectica. His live performances and curated sets have earned him recognition across both streaming and live platforms. Whether spinning at clubs, radio, or virtual events, Champs crafts unforgettable experiences rooted in immersive sound and dynamic flow.
