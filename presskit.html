BIO

Champs is an accomplished DJ based in Pittsburgh, PA who has been throwing parties, playing clubs and live-streaming on Twitch since 2019, delivering good vibes and high-energy experiences. A key figure in the online electronic music community, Champs has helped manage the Electric Daisy Carnival Discord Music Night LIVE since 2022, curating and broadcasting top-tier DJ sets to a global audience.

Stepping beyond the streaming world, Champs has established himself as a force in live music, playing venues in the area like Spirit Lodge, Bottlerocket Social Hall, THIS IS RED, and The Clement. He stays ahead of the curve by engaging with industry leaders, with his most recent event being the Pittsburgh DJ Summit in 2025.
