HEADSHOTS

BIO

Champs is an accomplished DJ based in Pittsburgh, PA who has been throwing parties, playing clubs and live-streaming on Twitch since 2019, delivering good vibes and high-energy experiences. A key figure in the online electronic music community, Champs has helped manage the Electric Daisy Carnival Discord Music Night LIVE since 2022, curating and broadcasting top-tier DJ sets to a global audience.

Stepping beyond the streaming world, Champs has established himself as a force in live music, playing venues in the Pittsburgh area like Spirit Lodge, Bottlerocket Social Hall, Jackworth, Pitch & Spigolo. He stays ahead of the curve by engaging with industry leading event DJs, with his most recent conference and networking event being the Pittsburgh DJ Summit in 2026.

As a former monthly resident on Ibiza Stardust Radio and current resident on Pittsburgh’s own MSYH.FM Radio, he showcases his deep passion for house and drum & bass. He also enjoys spinning trance & techno. He also doesn’t shy away from open-format DJing; including: pop, rap, hip-hop, rock, trap and even some country if the vibe is right.

Learn more about Champs on our About page!
