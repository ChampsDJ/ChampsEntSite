HOW TO BOOK & NEXT STEPS

Booking is easy! To get started, have a look on this page at our packages and add-ons. Then, simply visit our contact page and fill out our form with your event details and any add-ons you may be interested in.

From there, we will reply to you via email with a custom quote. If you decide to move forward, we will set up an initial consultation meeting. On that meeting, we will ask about your reception and wedding, how you see your day unfolding and how we can assist you in making your entertainment vision come to life. We want to hear about you and your love story, and how we can help tell that story with the entertainment during your wedding. We also can cover music selections and overall vibe you are going for, but if you don't have those selections nailed down that's okay, too. The purpose for this initial call is to make sure that we are the right fit for you and your wedding!

Shortly after the end of the call, we will provide you with a custom service contract that you can take and review before you get back to us! Once the contract is signed, your date is locked in and we will move forward with the planning phases from there!
