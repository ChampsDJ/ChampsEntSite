HOW TO BOOK & NEXT STEPS

Booking is easy! To get started, have a look on this page at our packages and add-ons. Then, simply visit our contact page and fill out our form with your event details and any add-ons you may be interested in.

From there, we will reply to you via email to gather more information and set up an initial proposal. Once the proposal is verbally accepted, we will schedule a call. On that call, we will ask about your music selections (must plays, nice to plays, do not plays) and overall vibe you are going for. We want to make sure that we are the right fit for you and your event!

Shortly after the end of the call, we will provide you with a custom service contract that you can take and review before you get back to us! Once the contract is signed, your date is locked in and we will go from there!
