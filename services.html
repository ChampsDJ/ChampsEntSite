SERVICES & PACKAGES

At Champs Entertainment, we offer a range of DJing, lighting, sound, livestreaming, recording, and equipment rental services tailored to your event's needs.

Whether you're hosting an intimate gathering or a large celebration, we have packages to fit any price range & occasion.

HOW TO BOOK & NEXT STEPS

Booking is easy! To get started, have a look on this page at our packages and add-ons. Then, simply visit our contact page and fill out our form with your event details and any packages/add-ons you may be interested in.

From there, we will reply to you via email to gather more information and set up a discovery call/Zoom. On that call, we will ask about your music selections (must plays, nice to plays, do not plays) and overall vibe you are going for. We want to make sure that we are the right fit for you, so we provide this call absolutely free!

At the end of the call, we will provide you with a custom proposal that you can take and review before you get back to us! Once the proposal is signed, your date is locked in and we will go from there!

We are booking 2025 & 2026 dates now, so don’t wait—reserve your date with us today!
