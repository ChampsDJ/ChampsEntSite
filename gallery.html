EVENT GALLERY

Below are sets, videos, and photos for events that we either have fully produced for our client or helped co-produce with other parties for a shared client.

Colombino Coffee Truck Sets

Champs played at and produced Pittsburgh's First Coffee Truck Party of 2025 at the Colombino Coffee Truck in Lawrenceville. We provided all equipment, sound, power (we ran the whole event off of a generator battery), and social media marketing, as well as played two sets, both of which are below.

This video is an excellent demonstration of our current livestream and recording capabilities.

Pittsburgh Against Apartheid

Champs participated in a local fundraiser event in February 2025, which we helped co-produce alongside DJs Against Apartheid and Not On Our Dime Pittsburgh. We were responsible for video & audio recording and the livestream. Champs also played a disco set, which is below.

This video is an excellent demonstration of our current livestream and recording capabilities.

Colombino Coffeehouse

Champs played at and produced Pittsburgh's First Coffeehouse Party of 2025 at Colombino Coffee on the North Side. We provided all equipment, sound, and social media marketing, as well as played a melodic techno set, which is below.

This video is an excellent demonstration of our current equipment, video & audio editing, and sound capabilities.
