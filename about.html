ABOUT

"I try to DJ like I'm playing a live instrument. I try to bring the energy and excitement of playing an instrument to what I do as a DJ." – Mix Master Mike

MY STORY ...

Since 2019, I’ve been DJing in clubs and streaming live on Twitch, bringing a unique set of skills that go beyond just playing music. I create memorable, immersive experiences, both virtually and in real life. That’s why I offer recording and livestreaming as part of my packages. If someone important can’t make it to your event, a livestream or recording ensures they can still be part of your special day, no matter where they are.

Beyond live events, I’ve been deeply involved in the online and Pittsburgh music communities:

Since 2022, I’ve helped manage EDC Discord Music Night LIVE, a monthly livestream showcasing DJ sets from the EDC Discord community. I help curate, broadcast, and host these sets on Twitch, ensuring each show reaches fans worldwide.

Starting in June 2024 and running for 12 legendary episodes, I was a monthly resident DJ on Ibiza Stardust Radio, the world’s largest dedicated house music radio station, based in Ibiza, Spain.

Since October 2024, I have been a regular at Pittsburgh Open Decks events at Spirit Hall, further honing my skills on club-standard equipment and continuing to play out live in front of audiences.

Beginning in March 2025, I’ve been a monthly resident on MSYH.FM Radio out of Pittsburgh, where I showcase my high-energy drum & bass mixes (not for the faint of heart).

In January 2025, I attended the Pittsburgh DJ Summit; a two-day DJ conference with presentations and workshops. At the conference, I connected with fellow DJs, industry professionals, and music enthusiasts to continue pushing my craft forward. Events like these are key to staying ahead of trends, learning from the best, and bringing fresh ideas and techniques to the decks.

But enough about me, what does this mean for you? It means you're getting a DJ with global experience, technical expertise, and a passion for making your event truly unforgettable.
